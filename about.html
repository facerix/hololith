About Hololith

Hololith is a stripped-down rendition of a popular Grimdark Future tabletop wargame, allowing you to test-drive army compositions, opponent counters, and more.

Built using standard web technologies as a Progressive Web App, it can be installed directly to your tablet/phone screen/desktop like a regular app, and it will behave more or less the same as one, and save its data in your device storage.

Credits:

Code by Rylee Corradini, with the assistance of a small army of LLM coding tools. Most art assets are from SVG Repo; placeholder animations were generated using SVGBackgrounds.com. Some 40k icons are from Warhammer40kGroup; the faction icons are from Certseeds. Additional 40k-flavor icons are from Locequen. Most were tweaked by me, because SVG is rad that way.
